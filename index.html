Sleep Health Checker is a free online tool designed to help you track and improve your sleeping patterns. Whether you struggle with insomnia, irregular bedtimes, or just want to make sure you’re getting enough rest, this tool provides quick and reliable insights. Enter your bedtime and wake-up time to instantly calculate your total sleep duration and get personalized recommendations.
